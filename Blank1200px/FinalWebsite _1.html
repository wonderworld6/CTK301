About Me

Hello, My name is Noelle Whitehead. I am a 3rd year college student majoring in Video Game Design looking to gain footing in the game creation world. I am very passionate about my work and the detail I put in. I spend almost every day and hour thinking of ways to explore and break barriers in todays concepts of gaming and online art. This is only the launching point for me right now. Growing up I had an obsession with gaming and older rendered models. So i strive to intertwine those aspects into my art and creations.
